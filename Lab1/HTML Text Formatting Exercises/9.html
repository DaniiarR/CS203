Letter of credit
a letter issued by a bank to another bank (especially one in a different country) to serve as a guarantee for payments made to a specified person under specified conditions.
Guarantee
a formal assurance (typically in writing) that certain conditions will be fulfilled, especially that a product will be repaired or replaced if not of a specified quality.
Apple
the round fruit of a tree of the rose family, which typically has thin green or red skin and crisp flesh.
Fork
an implement with two or more prongs used for lifting food to the mouth or holding it when cutting.
Scrambled eggs
a dish of eggs prepared by beating them with a little liquid and then cooking and stirring gently.
